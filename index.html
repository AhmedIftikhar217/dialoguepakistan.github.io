Dialogue Pakistan

A Public Policy Think Tank initiative

Dialogue Pakistan is the brainchild of Ahmed Iftikhar who aims to bring a positive change in Pakistan

Dialogue Pakistan is a Public Policy think tank initiative that aims to bring experts in Governance, Economy, Policy making to share their views on the current dicourse in Pakistan and present potential solutions

We firmly believe that change can not just be achieved by the governement or public office holders rather every citizen can play a part in building a better Pakistan
